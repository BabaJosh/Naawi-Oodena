The Pinnacle at Bridgwater

The Pinnacle at Bridgwater I is a 176-unit luxury apartment community that offers spacious one bedroom + den and two bedroom + den apartments for rent in the Bridgwater Trails neighbourhood of south Winnipeg.
 

With over 70 acres of open space, mature forest and lakes nearby in the Bridgwater Trails area, as well as amenities such as a grocery store, coffee shops, a doctor's office, a veterinary clinic and more, The Pinnacle is a vibrant apartment community that attracts premium tenants.

Key Details

The Pinnacle at Bridgwater consists of three, four-storey, garden-style walk-up buildings with a total of 176 units and a resident clubhouse.

This property is located in Bridgwater Trails, a new and growing community in southwest Winnipeg.

On-site amenities include a resident clubhouse, fitness centre, pool and other resident-focused services.

This fund is fully subscribed and no longer accepting subscriptions for investment.
